Installation

Get started in just 4 simple steps

1

Download & Extract

Download the ZIP file and extract it to a permanent location on your computer.

2

Open Extensions Page

Go to chrome://extensions/ in Chrome, edge://extensions/ in Edge, or brave://extensions/ in Brave.

3

Enable Developer Mode

Toggle the "Developer mode" switch in the top-right corner of the extensions page.

4

Load the Extension

Click "Load unpacked" and select the extracted folder containing manifest.json. Done!

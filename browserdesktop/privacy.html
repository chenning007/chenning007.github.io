Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 6, 2026

Overview

BrowserDesktop is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

Data Collection

This extension does NOT collect any data, including but not limited to:

  • Personal information
  • Browsing history
  • Search queries
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics
  • Cookies or tracking data

Data Storage

The extension stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Your selected search engine preference
  • Your last viewed background image URL
  • Your last selected image category
  • Weather cities preferences
  • Display settings (clock format, blur, opacity)

This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any server
  • Is not shared with third parties
  • Can be cleared by uninstalling the extension

Permissions Explained

Storage Permission

  • Purpose: To save your preferences locally
  • Data Stored: Search engine choice, last image URL, category preference, weather cities
  • Location: Local browser storage only

New Tab Override

  • Purpose: To display custom backgrounds on new tab pages
  • No data collection involved

Third-Party Services

The extension displays images from Unsplash.com:

  • Images are loaded from Unsplash's CDN servers
  • Unsplash may collect standard server logs as per their privacy policy
  • We do not control or have access to any data collected by Unsplash
  • Review Unsplash's privacy policy at: unsplash.com/privacy

The extension uses Open-Meteo for weather data:

  • Weather data is fetched from Open-Meteo's API
  • Only city coordinates are sent (no personal data)
  • Open-Meteo is a free, open-source weather API

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Review what data is stored locally
  • Clear all stored data by uninstalling the extension
  • Use the extension without providing any personal information

Compliance

This extension complies with:

  • Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
  • Microsoft Edge Add-ons Policies
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our landing page.
